2002-On-Cognitive-Informatics-Prof. Wang

Posted on 2019-04-09

Paper Title: “On Cognitive Informatics(CI), Prof. Yingxu Wang, 2002”
Highlights in this paper:

  1. The objectives of the study in CI:
    How we, as human beings, acquire, process, interpret and express information by the brain; and how we understand the minds of different individuals.
  2. The author proposed an information-matter-energy model (IME Model), in which he perceived that human beings live in two world: the abstract world (modeled by I) and the physical world (modeled by M and E), as shown in Fig.1.
    The significance of IME model or Why did the author propose this model?
    This IME model is developed by the author to describe a generic view towards the phsical and information worlds that we are living and resaerch.
    Modeling the absract world (Information) is still a foundamental issue yet to be explored and solved.
  3. The author also defined the CI:
    CI is an emerging disciplinary that studies the internal information processing mechanism of the brain and their engineering application, via an interdisciplinary approach.
